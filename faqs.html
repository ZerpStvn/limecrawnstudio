Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

Welcome to the Limecrawn FAQs page. Here you'll find answers to some of the most common questions about using Limecrawn. If you have any additional questions, feel free to contact our support team.

1. How do I create an account?

To create an account, tap the "Sign Up" button on the Limecrawn app's main screen. Follow the instructions to provide your email address, choose a username, and set a password. Once completed, you can start exploring our manga collection.

2. How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive instructions on how to reset your password.

3. How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information by navigating to your account settings within the app. Here, you can modify your display name, email address, and other personal details.

4. How do I report an issue or provide feedback?

If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, please use the in-app support feature or contact us directly at info@limecrawnstudio.com. We value your feedback and continuously work to improve your experience.

5. How do I connect with the community?

Limecrawn features community areas where you can share comments, join discussions, and review manga. Simply navigate to the community section of the app and join the conversation.

6. How do I explore manga content on Limecrawn?

You can use the search function or browse curated categories on Limecrawn to explore manga titles. Filter results by genre, popularity, or new releases to find content that suits your interests.

7. What devices are supported by Limecrawn?

Limecrawn is designed to work on a variety of devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. We support both Android and iOS platforms (Comming Soon), as well as web access via modern browsers.

8. What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

If you experience any technical issues while using Limecrawn, try restarting the app. If the problem persists, please contact our support team at info@limecrawnstudio.com with a detailed description of the issue.

If your question wasn't answered here, please feel free to reach out to our support team for further assistance.
