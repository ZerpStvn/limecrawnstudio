Community Guidelines

Last Updated: December 24, 2024

Welcome to Limecrawn! We are proud to bring together a community of manga and reading enthusiasts. To ensure a positive and engaging experience for everyone, we ask that you follow these Community Guidelines when using our service.

1. Respect and Courtesy

We expect all community members to treat one another with respect. Please:

  • Engage in constructive and respectful discussions.
  • Avoid harassing, bullying, or threatening language.
  • Refrain from using hate speech, slurs, or discriminatory remarks.
  • Respect othersâ€™ opinions and cultural differences.

2. Appropriate Content

We encourage sharing creativity and insights about manga and related content. When contributing to our community, please:

  • Share content that is appropriate for a general audience.
  • Clearly mark or restrict explicit content according to our guidelines.
  • Only share material that you own or have permission to use, respecting copyright and intellectual property rights.
  • Avoid posting spam, irrelevant advertisements, or promotional content not related to our community.

3. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited on Limecrawn:

  • Posting or distributing illegal content or unauthorized copyrighted materials.
  • Engaging in spam, phishing, or other disruptive behaviors.
  • Sharing personal information about others without their consent.
  • Any behavior that compromises the safety, security, or integrity of the community.

4. User Contributions

Your contributions help create a thriving community. When posting reviews, comments, or other content:

  • Ensure your content is constructive, clear, and on-topic.
  • Maintain a friendly tone even when disagreeing with others.
  • Avoid inflammatory language and personal attacks.

5. Reporting Violations

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it using our in-app reporting feature or contact our support team at info@limecrawnstudio.com. Your reports will be reviewed, and appropriate actions will be taken.

6. Enforcement

We reserve the right to take appropriate action if these guidelines are violated. Possible enforcement measures include:

  • Removing or editing offending content.
  • Issuing warnings or temporary suspensions of user accounts.
  • Permanent bans from Limecrawn for repeated or severe violations.

7. Updates to the Guidelines

These Community Guidelines may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, we will update the "Last Updated" date and notify the community as needed. Continued use of Limecrawn constitutes your acceptance of any updated guidelines.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding these guidelines, please feel free to contact us at info@limecrawnstudio.com.

Thank you for helping us maintain a positive and engaging community at Limecrawn!
